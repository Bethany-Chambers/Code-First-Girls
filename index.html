A bit about me and baking

Hi, I'm Beth and welcome to Beth's Bakes! I'm a keen and budding baker located in Leeds. This all started with my love for baking and then baking for all of my friends and family. And from there, it grew into what you see today!

  • I started baking from age 4
  • I started of making cakes with my nan's and from there my passion grew
  • I went to the NEC for a cake exhibition and my eyes were opened into the world of extraordinary cakes!
  • I bought some equipment, went back home and got creating
  • From there, I taught myself everything I know (with help from YouTube of course!)
  • I've been growing and love taking on a challenge
